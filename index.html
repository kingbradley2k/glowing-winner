What is included in the free trial?

The free trial includes access to all core training modules, phishing simulations, and basic reporting features. You can explore the platform and see how it works for your organization.

Yes, our Enterprise plan allows for full customization of training content. You can tailor the modules to fit your organization's specific needs and industry requirements.

We update our training content quarterly to reflect the latest cybersecurity threats and best practices. This ensures your employees are always learning the most relevant information.
